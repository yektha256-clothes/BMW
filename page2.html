Ithu vaayikkumbol nee enthaayaalum onnu chindikkum. "Ivanu ithu enthu patti?" ennu nee ippo chinthikkunnundavum. Pakshe satyam parayaallo, chilappol ee pottatharangalum, nammude ee thallukalum okke illayirunnuvenkil ente jeevitham oru architect illatha site pole aayene. Ninne pole oru kuthirane kittiyadh ente bhagyam aanennaanu njaan ippo karuthunnath. Kaaranam:
  • Nammude logic illatha samsaarangal.
  • Ninte aa chiriyum, pinne idakkidakkulla aa mandatharangalum.
  • Ellaathinum appuram, enne sahikkunnu enna ninte aa "valiya manassu".
Pakshe sariyaayittu paranjal, ee lokathil vere aaru vannaalum ninne pole enne santhosharippikkaan kazhiyilla. Ninte mssg illatha oru divasam polum ippo enikku chinthikkaan vayya. Athukondu thanneyaanu ninne njaan snehichu poyath. Ini enthu vannalum, namukku ee oru vibe pidich munnottu pokaam . Insha Allah ❤️
